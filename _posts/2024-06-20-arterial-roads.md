This is a good example of repormance of effectiveness measurement in public infrastructure projects. While there were shorfcomings in the data collection, we were able to conclude that benefits of the initiatives were not demonstrable. The analisis involved reviewing traffic data, before and after the implementation of the initiatives while controlling for confounding variables such as seasonal traffic variations.

For details see [the full report](https://www.audit.vic.gov.au/report/effectiveness-arterial-road-congestion-initiatives)
