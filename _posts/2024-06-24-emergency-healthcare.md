The Department of Health publishes performance data on emergency healthcare services, but it is not disaggregated by demographic factors. The Data Science team carried out this disaggregation to identify whether vulnerable groups are experiencing different levels of access to emergency healthcare. We found some unexpected and counterintuitive relationships.

For details see [the full report](https://www.audit.vic.gov.au/report/access-emergency-healthcare).
